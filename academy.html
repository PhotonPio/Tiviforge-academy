NEM 3.0 / Net Billing Tariff Deep Dive
2026 Updated
+

NEM 3.0 changed everything. Export credits dropped to ~$0.05–$0.08/kWh avoided cost (versus $0.25–$0.45 under NEM 2.0). This is why batteries are now essential for most systems. Learn to explain it in plain English, handle objections, and use it to close more battery adders legally and ethically.

California Codes, Compliance & CSLB HIS
Required
+

California law requires CSLB Home Improvement Salesperson (HIS) registration for anyone who solicits, negotiates, or sells home improvement contracts — including solar. We walk you through the exact registration process, what to say in the field, the Solar Energy System Disclosure Document requirements (16-pt bold), the 3-day right of rescission, and senior protections.
