Important Disclosures

Partnership: TiviForge is an authorized sales partner in the Axia by Qcells network. All solar system designs and contracts are reviewed and executed by properly licensed California contractors. TiviForge does not hold a contractor's license.

Earnings: Training, calculators, and redline pricing are for educational and estimation purposes only. Earnings are not guaranteed and depend entirely on individual effort, market conditions, and closing ability.

CSLB HIS Requirement: All 1099 independent contractors who solicit, negotiate, or sell home improvement contracts in California must obtain and maintain valid CSLB Home Improvement Salesperson (HIS) registration. TiviForge provides guidance but does not guarantee approval. CSLB.gov

NEM 3.0 Accuracy: Net Billing Tariff (NEM 3.0) rules, export rates, and incentives are current as of March 2026. Subject to change by CPUC at any time. Always verify current rates with the applicable utility and CPUC.

Solar Disclosure: California law requires the Solar Energy System Disclosure Document be provided to homeowners in 16-pt bold before signing. The Home Solicitation Sales Act provides a 3-day right of rescission. TiviForge provides compliance training — all reps are responsible for following applicable law.

Tesla Powerwall 3 Pricing: Battery pricing of $11,500/unit is an Axia partnership estimate as of Q1 2026. Verify current pricing with the Axia partner team before quoting customers. SGIP incentives, where applicable, may reduce effective cost.
