About & Legal

Built by a Rep
For Reps

TiviForge was built by a California solar salesperson who got tired of watching talented reps work hard for companies that hid pricing, inflated redlines, and left their teams with no real training — just a script and a prayer.

After securing an authorized partnership in the Axia by Qcells network, TiviForge now offers 1099 independent contractors a real opportunity: a transparent redline, professional tools, and actual education on how California solar works in 2026.

We are not a solar contractor. We are a sales partnership and training academy. All system designs are reviewed and finalized by properly licensed Axia by Qcells contractors. All 1099 reps operate as independent contractors under California law, including required CSLB HIS registration.

Questions? Reach us at academy@tiviforge.com or through the AI Solar Coach widget below.

Important Disclosures

Partnership: TiviForge is an authorized sales partner in the Axia by Qcells network. All solar system designs and contracts are reviewed and executed by properly licensed California contractors. TiviForge does not hold a contractor's license.

Earnings: Training, calculators, and redline pricing are for educational and estimation purposes only. Earnings are not guaranteed and depend entirely on individual effort, market conditions, and closing ability. Past performance of other reps is not indicative of your results.

CSLB HIS Requirement: All 1099 independent contractors who solicit, negotiate, or sell home improvement contracts in California must obtain and maintain valid CSLB Home Improvement Salesperson (HIS) registration. TiviForge provides guidance but does not guarantee approval. CSLB.gov

NEM 3.0 Accuracy: Net Billing Tariff (NEM 3.0) rules, export rates, and incentives are current as of March 2026. Subject to change by CPUC at any time. Always verify current rates with the applicable utility and CPUC.

Solar Disclosure: California law requires the Solar Energy System Disclosure Document be provided to homeowners in 16-pt bold before signing. The Home Solicitation Sales Act provides a 3-day right of rescission. TiviForge provides compliance training — all reps are responsible for following applicable law.
